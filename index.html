Our Mission

Transforming Diabetes Care

DiabetesConnect addresses the diabetes epidemic nationwide with a focus on medically underserved and rural communities. We recognize that managing diabetes shouldn't mean navigating complex healthcare systems alone.


Our mission is to connect patients to existing resources, providing peer support, and ensuring no one faces health management in isolation. Available in English and Spanish, we meet you where you are in your journey.

About Us

Empowering Communities

Diabetes Connect, founded in the Inland Empire, is dedicated to reducing health disparities in underserved communities by empowering individuals with diabetes through education, resources, and community support.

Through partnerships with certified diabetes educators, nutritionists, and healthcare professionals, we deliver evidence-based care combined with personalized support.

We're building a future where care is accessible, supportive, and empowering for every individual in our community.
