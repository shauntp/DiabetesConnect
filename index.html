Our Mission

Transforming Diabetes Care

DiabetesConnect addresses the diabetes epidemic nationwide with a focus on California's Inland Empire. We recognize that managing diabetes shouldn't mean navigating complex healthcare systems alone.

Our mission is connecting patients to existing resources, providing peer support, and ensuring no one faces diabetes management in isolation. Available in English and Spanish, we meet you where you are in your journey.

About Us

Empowering Communities

We are a dedicated team of UC Riverside undergraduate students committed to reducing health disparities in the Inland Empire and beyond. Our passion drives us to create meaningful change in diabetes care accessibility.

Through partnerships with certified diabetes educators, nutritionists, and healthcare professionals, we deliver evidence-based care combined with personalized support.

We're building a future where diabetes care is accessible, supportive, and empowering for every individual in our community.
