Our Mission

DiabetesConnect addresses the diabetes care crisis in Riverside County and the Inland Empire. We recognize that having diabetes is challenging enough without having to navigate complex healthcare systems alone.

Our team connects patients to existing diabetes care resources, provides peer support, and ensures no one faces diabetes management in isolation. We work with local healthcare providers, community organizations, and volunteers to create a comprehensive support network.

Available in English and Spanish, our services meet you where you are in your diabetes journey.

About DiabetesConnect

We are a team of UCR undergraduates who want to help reduce health disparities in the Inland Empire. To achieve this, we partnered with certified diabetes educators, nutritionists, and healthcare professionals dedicated to helping you manage your diabetes with confidence and ease.

Our comprehensive approach combines evidence-based care with personalized support to provide you with the tools and resources you need to live a healthy, fulfilling life with diabetes.

We're committed to making diabetes care accessible, supportive, and empowering for everyone in our community.
